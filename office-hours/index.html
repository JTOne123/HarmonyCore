Harmony Core Office Hours

The Harmony Core development team meets about every 6 weeks to give users an opportunity to ask questions and give feedback on the Harmony Core project. Join us for office hours hosted via GoToWebinar. You can drop in to ask our experts your burning questions, get the latest updates, and hear how other customers are implementing Harmony Core in their Synergy applications.
